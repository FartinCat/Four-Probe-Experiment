Instruments Required
  • Four-Probe Apparatus (probe unit with spring-loaded tungsten tips)
  • Constant Current Source — DC, regulated (0 – 200 mA)
  • Digital Millivoltmeter / Nanovoltmeter (resolution: 0.001 mV)
  • Digital Milliammeter (resolution: 0.01 mA)
  • Thermocouple (type K) with digital temperature indicator (±0.5°C)
  • Programmable Oven / Furnace (25°C – 250°C) with PID temperature controller
Components & Materials
  • Silicon semiconductor specimen (p-type chip / wafer)
  • Four collinear tungsten probe tips (equal spacing, spring-loaded)
  • Probe holder with micrometer-controlled vertical adjustment
  • Sample holder with integrated heater coil
  • Connecting leads — 4 mm banana plugs, colour-coded
  • Insulating base plate (Teflon / alumina)

Circuit Schematic (Description)

The circuit consists of two independent loops:

  • Current Loop: Constant Current Source → P1 (outer) → Silicon Specimen → P4 (outer) → back to source.
  • Voltage Loop: Digital Millivoltmeter connected across P2 and P3 (inner probes). High input impedance of voltmeter ensures negligible current through voltage probes.
⚠ The probes must be arranged in strict collinear order: P1 – P2 – P3 – P4, with equal spacing s between adjacent probes.
