Formula Reference Sheet
Step 1 — Unit Conversion
V (V) = V (mV) × 10⁻³    I (A) = I (mA) × 10⁻³    T (K) = T (°C) + 273.15
Step 2 — Resistance
R (Ω) = V (V) / I (A)
Step 3 — Resistivity (thin specimen, W/s < 0.5)
ρ (Ω·cm) = R × π × W / ln(2)    [π = 3.14159, ln 2 = 0.69315, W in cm]
Step 4 — Natural Log & Inverse Temperature
ln(ρ) = loge(ρ)     1/T × 10³ = 1000 / T (K)
Step 5 — Band Gap from Slope
slopeK = slopegraph × 10³    Eg = 2 × k × |slopeK| = 2 × 8.617×10⁻⁵ × |slopeK|  eV
Physical Constants
